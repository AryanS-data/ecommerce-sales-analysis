# Key Findings

- Total Revenue generated: **10.67 Million**
- Total Profit generated: **1.84 Million**
- Overall Profit Margin: **17.29%**
- Total Orders Processed: **4,000**
- Electronics contributed approximately **50%** of total revenue.
- Accessories generated nearly **40%** of total sales.
- Office products contributed approximately **10%** of total revenue.
- West region recorded the highest sales.
- North region recorded the lowest sales.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Continue strengthening the Electronics category through targeted marketing.
- Improve promotional strategies for Office products.
- Explore growth opportunities in the North region.
- Maintain inventory for consistently high-selling products.
- Continue monitoring sales trends to support business decisions.
